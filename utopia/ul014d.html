Dialogue following No. 14


King: Now, then for our first Drawing-Room. Where are the Princesses? What an extraordinary thing it is that since European looking-glasses have been supplied to the Royal bedrooms my daughters are invariably late!

Lord Dramaleigh: Sir, their Royal Highnesses await your pleasure in the Ante-room.

King: Oh. Then request them to do us the favour to enter at once.
